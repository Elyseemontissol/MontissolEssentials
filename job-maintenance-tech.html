Job Overview

About This Role

Montissol Essentials LLC is hiring a Maintenance Technician to support equipment upkeep, supply management, and light facility maintenance at government and commercial job sites. You will ensure that cleaning equipment is operational, consumable inventories are maintained, and minor facility repairs are handled promptly. This role is key to keeping our operations running smoothly and on schedule.

Responsibilities

  • Inspect, maintain, and perform minor repairs on cleaning equipment including vacuums, scrubbers, buffers, and extraction units
  • Track consumable inventory (chemicals, paper products, trash liners) and coordinate restocking with the Site Supervisor
  • Perform light facility maintenance tasks such as changing light bulbs, tightening fixtures, patching minor wall damage, and unclogging drains
  • Maintain an organized equipment storage area and ensure all tools are accounted for
  • Log all maintenance activities, equipment repairs, and inventory usage in daily reports
  • Coordinate with vendors for equipment service and parts procurement as needed
  • Support setup and teardown for special cleaning projects or site transitions
  • Follow all safety procedures and wear required PPE during maintenance tasks

Qualifications

  • Minimum 1 year of experience in equipment maintenance, facility maintenance, or a related hands-on role
  • Basic mechanical aptitude and ability to troubleshoot common equipment issues
  • Experience with inventory tracking and supply chain coordination preferred
  • Ability to read equipment manuals and follow manufacturer maintenance schedules
  • Physically capable of lifting up to 60 lbs and working in various facility environments
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail in documentation
  • Must pass a background check (required for government facility access)
  • Valid driver's license preferred; high school diploma or equivalent required
