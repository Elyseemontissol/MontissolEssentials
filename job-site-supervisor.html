Job Overview

About This Role

Montissol Essentials LLC is hiring a Site Supervisor to oversee daily cleaning and facility support operations at government and commercial sites. You will lead a team of technicians, manage schedules, enforce safety standards, and serve as the primary point of contact between our crew and the client. This role requires strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to maintain compliance with contract requirements.

Responsibilities

  • Supervise and direct janitorial and industrial cleaning crews during assigned shifts
  • Assign daily tasks and ensure all work areas are completed to contract specifications
  • Conduct pre-shift briefings covering safety, priorities, and site-specific requirements
  • Verify completion of QA/QC checklists and sign off on daily documentation
  • Coordinate site access, badging, and security protocols for all team members
  • Manage supply inventory and submit requisition requests as needed
  • Address client concerns promptly and communicate status updates to management
  • Enforce PPE requirements and safety procedures; conduct incident reporting when necessary
  • Train new hires on site procedures, equipment operation, and documentation standards

Qualifications

  • Minimum 2 years of supervisory experience in janitorial, custodial, or facility services
  • Experience working in government, military, or regulated facility environments preferred
  • Strong leadership and communication skills with the ability to manage a team of 5-15 personnel
  • Proficiency with quality-control documentation and inspection procedures
  • Familiar with OSHA safety standards and site-specific compliance requirements
  • Must pass a background check and obtain required security clearances for site access
  • Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
  • High school diploma or equivalent required; additional education or certifications a plus
