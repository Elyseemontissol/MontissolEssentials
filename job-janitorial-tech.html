Job Overview

About This Role

Montissol Essentials LLC is seeking a dependable Janitorial Technician to perform daily cleaning and sanitation tasks at government and commercial facilities. This role is essential to maintaining safe, hygienic, and inspection-ready environments. You will follow documented procedures, complete quality-control checklists, and uphold the standards required by our facility contracts.

Responsibilities

  • Clean and sanitize restrooms, break rooms, lobbies, offices, and common areas according to site-specific procedures
  • Sweep, mop, vacuum, and buff floors in assigned zones
  • Empty trash and recycling receptacles and replace liners
  • Restock supplies including paper products, soap, and sanitizer
  • Complete daily quality-control checklists and report any maintenance issues
  • Follow all safety protocols, including proper use of PPE and chemical handling procedures
  • Maintain cleaning equipment in good working order and report deficiencies
  • Support periodic deep-cleaning tasks as scheduled by the Site Supervisor

Qualifications

  • Prior janitorial or custodial experience preferred; on-the-job training provided
  • Ability to follow written procedures and complete documentation accurately
  • Reliable attendance and punctuality
  • Ability to lift up to 50 lbs and perform physical tasks including standing, bending, and walking for extended periods
  • Must pass a background check (required for government facility access)
  • Basic understanding of cleaning chemicals and safety data sheets (SDS) is a plus
  • Evening and weekend availability may be required depending on site assignment
