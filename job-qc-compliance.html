Job Overview

About This Role

Montissol Essentials LLC is seeking a QC & Compliance Coordinator to manage quality assurance programs and ensure regulatory compliance across our government and commercial facility contracts. You will develop and maintain inspection systems, track performance metrics, coordinate audit preparation, and serve as the primary quality resource for site teams. This is a key role for ensuring our services consistently meet or exceed contract standards.

Responsibilities

  • Develop, implement, and maintain quality-control plans, inspection checklists, and standard operating procedures
  • Conduct regular site audits and inspections to verify compliance with contract performance standards
  • Track and analyze quality metrics including deficiency rates, corrective action timelines, and client satisfaction
  • Coordinate corrective and preventive action (CAPA) processes when deficiencies are identified
  • Prepare documentation for government contract audits, performance reviews, and compliance reporting
  • Train site supervisors, crew leads, and technicians on QC procedures and documentation requirements
  • Liaise with clients, contracting officers, and government representatives on quality matters
  • Maintain organized records of all inspection reports, training logs, and compliance documentation
  • Stay current on relevant regulations, industry standards, and best practices for facility services

Qualifications

  • Minimum 3 years of experience in quality control, compliance, or quality assurance in facility services or a related field
  • Experience working with government contracts (federal, state, or municipal) strongly preferred
  • Knowledge of Quality Management System (QMS) principles and documentation practices
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret performance data and identify trends
  • Excellent written communication skills for report writing and procedure documentation
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office and familiarity with inspection or compliance tracking software
  • Must pass a background check and obtain required clearances for government facility access
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience preferred; certifications such as ASQ CQI or similar are a plus
